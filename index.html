Backstreet Boys
Backstreet Boys has been an American vocal band that was formed in 1993. The band comprises 5 people including Nick Carter, Howi Dorough, Brian Littrell, Kevin Richardson, and AJ McLean. The band was just a group of ordinary singers up until 1996 when the band got fame due to their debut international album named Backstreet Boys. We can deduce that the Backstreet Boys has been one of the top bands when it comes to American music.
