Backstreet Boys

The famous Backstreet Boys is an American vocal band that was formed in 1993. This is one of the most popular boy bands in the 90's. The band members are Nick Carter, Howi Dorough, Brian Littrell, Kevin Richardson, and AJ McLean. The band was just a group of ordinary singers up until 1996 when the band got fame due to their debut international album named Backstreet Boys. It is hard to deny the fact that the Backstreet Boys has been one of the top bands when it comes to American music.
