CHRISTMAS IN JULY! BACKSTREET BOYS ANNOUNCE FIRST-EVER CHRISTMAS ALBUM


IT’S CHRISTMAS IN JULY AS BACKSTREET BOYS ANNOUNCE HIGHLY ANTICIPATED FIRST-EVER CHRISTMAS ALBUM ‘A VERY BACKSTREET CHRISTMAS’ COMING OCTOBER 14TH


Christmas comes early this year as the Backstreet Boys announce their highly anticipated Christmas album ‘A Very Backstreet Christmas’ (BMG) will be released on October 14th, 2022. “We’ve been wanting to do a Christmas album for nearly 30 years now and we’re beyond excited that it’s finally happening,” Howie Dorough shares, “We had such a fun experience putting our BSB twist on some of our favorite Christmas classics and can’t wait to be part of our fans’ holiday season.” ‘A Very Backstreet Christmas’ is the Backstreet Boys’ first ever Christmas album and will feature timeless holiday classics such as “White Christmas”, “Silent Night”, and “Have Yourself A Merry Little Christmas” plus three all new original holiday songs “Christmas In New York,” “Together,” and “Happy Days.”
