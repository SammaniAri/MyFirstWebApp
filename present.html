It's christmas in July as Backstreet Boys announce highly anticipated first-ever christmas album 'A Very Backstreet Christmas' coming October 14th, 2022.


‘A Very Backstreet Christmas’ is the Backstreet Boys’ first ever Christmas album and will feature timeless holiday classics such as “White Christmas”, “Silent Night”, and “Have Yourself A Merry Little Christmas” plus three all new original holiday songs “Christmas In New York,” “Together,” and “Happy Days.”
